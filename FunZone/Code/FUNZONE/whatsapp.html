Adventure bay is a virtual splash factory for the entire family. Minimum 7 water slides and other water features such as tipping cones, hose jets, water guns and loads of other fun-filled features! Hours of fun for kids of all ages!

Found mostly in amusement parks as a continuous loop, it is a popular leisure activity.
